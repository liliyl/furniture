About Furniture Seeker

Find a furniture you love online but out of your budget?

No worries! Furniture Seeker is here to help you find similar but much more wallet-friendly ones!

Pick a Category

Pick one from the 8 furniture categories. These categories should cover a fully functional home sweet home with a minimalist lifestyle.

Image and/or Description

Copy and paste the image URL and/or description from the product page of the furniture you like. For it to work the best, the image is preferred to have a white background.

Price limit? Sure!

You can also enter a price limit if you wish.

Submit and Enjoy

Just hit "submit" and have fun shopping without breaking your wallet!
